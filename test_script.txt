# Cultural Breakdown of India

## Introduction
India is a diverse country with rich cultural heritage.
• Home to multiple religions, languages, and traditions
• Known for its unity in diversity

## Regional Cultures
### North India
• Influenced by Mughal culture
• Known for its rich cuisine
• Traditional music and dance forms

### South India
• Strong classical traditions
• Temple architecture
• Unique film industry

## Modern India
• Blend of traditional and contemporary values
• Growing technology sector
• Evolving social norms
